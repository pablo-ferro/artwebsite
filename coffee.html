A series of paintings made with coffee, caffeine stains and that smell that feels like home.
This collection is about gratitude and love. And flowers.

I will pay both:

To those surrounded by flowers, to my joy and the rest too, alive, I painted you with a huge respect, slow songs and memory for what you did. You saved my life, looked after me, hold my hand, my hopes, my heart. You treated with an infinite patience. I picked the flowers for you, you taught me to gift flowers, always. To carry my regrets and feeligns forever as a sign of rooting in reality, to blossom regardless of the fact that the water needed came from my own tears.
I owe you my life, since you saved it.

The last sip

I thank those that shared time with me, in the same table, under the same light and music. I promise I always looked at your eyes when you were talking, and sometimes I found your soul.
